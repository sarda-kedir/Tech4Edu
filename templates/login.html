About Tech4Edu

Offline-first learning for every classroom.

Across many Ethiopian classrooms, students face the same barrier: learning tools disappear the moment connectivity drops. Lessons are missed, revision stalls, and teachers struggle to track progress when resources are scattered across paper notes, inconsistent access, and unreliable internet.

Tech4Edu solves that by putting a complete learning experience directly in students' hands. The platform stores lesson content offline, generates PDFs for study without data, and keeps quizzes and feedback available at any time. Progress tracking runs locally, so students and teachers can see growth even when the connection is down.

We go further by adding AI-guided tutoring and step-by-step explanations to reinforce understanding instead of memorization. Students can revise for exams, catch up on missed classes, or explore new topics with a clear path, steady practice, and visible improvement.
